
<h1>Business Understanding</h1>

<h2>Project Background</h2>
<p>The Superstore company operates across four regions: West, East, Central, and South. While the company has experienced overall sales growth over time, regional sales performance remains uneven.
The West and East regions consistently generate higher sales compared with the South region, creating a need to understand the factors contributing to this performance gap.</p>

<h2>Business Problem</h2>
<p>The South region is significantly underperforming compared with the West and East regions. Management needs to identify the factors behind this gap, understand what successful regions are doing differently, and uncover opportunities to improve sales performance in the South region.</p>

<h2>Project Objectives</h2>
<p>The objective of this analysis is to investigate the drivers of regional sales performance and identify the factors contributing to South region underperformance.</p>

<h2>Business Questions</h2>
<p>The analysis seeks to answer the following questions:</p>
<ol>
<li>What is the total revenue generated by the company? </li>
<li>Which regions generate the highest and lowest sales? </li>
<li>Which product categories and sub-categories contribute the most revenue? </li>
<li>Which customer segments contribute the most sales? </li>
<li>How has sales performance changed over time? </li>
<li>Are there seasonal patterns in sales performance? </li>
<li>Why is the South region underperforming compared with the West and East regions?</li>
</ol>
