<h1>Exploratory And Descriptive Analysis</h1>
<p>This phase focused on understanding overall business performance, answering business questions and identifying patterns that require further investigation.</p>

<h2>Business Questions</h2>
<p>The analysis seeks to answer the following questions:</p>
<ol>
<li>What is the total revenue generated by the company? </li>
<li>Which regions generate the highest and lowest sales?</li> 
<li>Which top 10 cities generate the highest sales?</li>
<li>Which product categories and sub-categories contribute the most revenue?</li> 
<li>Which customer segments contribute the most sales?</li>
<li>How has sales performance changed over time? </li>
<li>Are there seasonal patterns in sales performance?</li>
</ol>

<h2>Sales Overview: finding</h2>
<p>The company generated strong overall sales which is $2 261 537, with clear differences in performance across regions.</p>
<h2>Regional performance: finding</h2>
<p>Regional sales performance showed that:</p>
<ul>
<li>West generated $710,220 sales</li>
<li>East generated $669,519 sales</li>
<li>Central generated $492,647 sales</li>
<li>South generated $389,151 sales</li>
</ul>
<p>The South region was identified as the lowest-performing region, forming the basis for further diagnostic investigation.
Product performance finding
Technology was the highest-performing category, with strong contributions from several sub-categories.
Customer segment: finding
The Consumer segment generated the highest overall sales, indicating that individual customers represent the largest source of company revenue.</p>
<h2>Sales trend analysis</h2>
<h3>Yearly performance: finding</h3>
<p>Sales increased consistently from 2015 to 2018, indicating positive business growth.</p>
<h3>Monthly performance: finding</h3>

<p>A clear seasonal pattern was identified:</p>
<ul>
<li>November and December recorded the highest sales.</li>
<li>January and February recorded the lowest sales.</li>
</ul>
