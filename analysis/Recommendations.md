<h1>Recommendations</h1>
<h2>Recommendation 1: Increase Customer Acquisition and Order Volume</h2>

<p>The business should focus on attracting new customers and encouraging more frequent purchases in the South region to address the lower order volume contributing to underperformance. This may be achieved through targeted marketing campaigns, promotional initiatives, and customer engagement strategies.</p>

<h2>Recommendation 2: Leverage Strong Customer Spending Behavior</h2>

<p>Since South customers already demonstrate relatively high spending per order, the business should prioritize expanding customer reach and transaction volume while maintaining pricing strategies that support current order values.</p>

<h2>Recommendation 3: Strengthen Category Performance Across the Product Portfolio</h2>

<p>As South underperforms across all major product categories, improvement efforts should focus on enhancing category visibility, evaluating regional differences in inventory and promotional effectiveness, and adopting successful strategies used in higher-performing regions.<p>

<h2>Executive Recommendation Summary</h2>

<p>To improve performance in the South region, the business should focus on increasing customer acquisition and order volume, leveraging the region's strong customer spending behavior, and strengthening sales performance across all product categories. These initiatives can help close the sales gap and drive sustainable regional growth.</p>
