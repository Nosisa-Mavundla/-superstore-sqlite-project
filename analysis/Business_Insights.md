<h1>Business Insights</h1>
<h2>Insight 1: South Faces a Customer Demand Challenge</h2>
<p> The  South region generates fewer orders than its counterparts, limiting the number of revenue-generating transactions and contributing to lower overall sales performance.</p>

<h2>Insight 2: Customer Spending Is Not the Primary Driver of Underperformance</h2>
<p>South customers exhibit relatively strong spending behavior, suggesting that increasing transaction volume is likely to have a greater impact on revenue growth than increasing order value.</p>

<h2>Insight 3: Sales Weakness Is Broad-Based Across Product Categories</h2>
<p>The sales gap is observed across all major product categories, indicating that South's underperformance is a regional issue rather than a category-specific problem.</p>
