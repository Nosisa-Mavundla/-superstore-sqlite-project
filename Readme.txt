Superstore Sales Insights Dashboard
Overview
This project is an interactive sales analytics dashboard developed to analyze sales performance across different business dimensions, including regions, product categories, customer segments, and time. The dashboard enables users to explore business performance through interactive filters and provides insights into the factors driving regional sales differences.
Live Dashboard: https://superstore-sales-insights.streamlit.app/

Business Problem
The company generates sales across four regions: West, East, Central, and South. Although the business performs well overall, the South region consistently records the lowest sales.This project investigates the factors contributing to South's underperformance by analyzing sales patterns, customer purchasing behaviour, product performance, and seasonal trends.

Objectives
The project aims to:
Analyze overall business performance.
Identify regional sales trends.
Determine the factors contributing to South's lower sales.
Provide data-driven business recommendations.
Build an interactive dashboard for business users.

Trend analysis

Key findings:
West generated the highest overall sales.
South consistently recorded the lowest sales.
Lower order volume—not lower spending per customer—was the primary driver of South's underperformance.
Technology was the highest-performing product category.
Sales exhibited seasonal peaks toward the end of the year.

Business Recommendations
Increase customer acquisition initiatives in the South region.
Strengthen marketing campaigns during high-demand periods.
Expand successful Technology offerings.
Monitor regional performance using interactive dashboards.
Strengthen Category Performance Across the Product Portfolio
